About the band

Stories, drinkin’ songs, and battle cries.

The Hounds of Cuchulain are a Celtic folk band blending traditional reels, jigs and ballads with gritty, modern songwriting. Rooted in the music of Ireland and Scotland, the band leans into big choruses, rowdy singalongs and moments of delicate storytelling.

From intimate pubs to full theatres and festival stages, the Hounds deliver high-energy sets that feel like a session and a show all at once.

  • Full band with traditional Celtic instrumentation
  • Repertoire of originals plus beloved standards
  • Flexible sets for pubs, concerts, festivals & events
